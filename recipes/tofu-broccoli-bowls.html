Tofu Broccoli Bowls

Description

This nourishing bowl is one of my favorite feel-good meals. It's packed with brown rice, crispy tofu, roasted broccoli, and a zingy carrot-ginger dressing.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Brown Rice
  • Baked Tofu
  • Carrot Ginger Dressing

Steps

  1. Assemble each bowl with the rice, broccoli and tofu.
  2. Drizzle with the dressing.
